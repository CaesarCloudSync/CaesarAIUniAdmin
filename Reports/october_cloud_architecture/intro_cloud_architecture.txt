Cloud Computing
---------------
Cloud computing has become a transformative force in the IT industry, offering businesses and individuals a new way to access and use computing resources. 
Understanding the key concepts of cloud computing is essential for harnessing its benefits and making informed decisions about its adoption.

NIST Cloud Computing Characteristics and Their Involvement
-----------------------------------------------------------

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) has defined five essential characteristics of cloud computing: on-demand self-service, broad network access, resource pooling, rapid elasticity, and measured service. 
These characteristics are fundamental to the cloud computing model and play a crucial role in its adoption and use.
